name: Button

category: Styles

Buttons can be used with <a>, <button>, and <input> elements.

Types of buttons:

  • button-white: Standard button
  • button-orange: Provides extra visual weight and identifies the primary action in a set of buttons
  • orange-button: Indicates a successful or positive action
  • solid-orange-button: Indicates a successful or positive action
  • blue-button: Indicates a successful or positive action
  • turquoise-button: Indicates a successful or positive action
  • white-button: Indicates a successful or positive action
  • darkblue-button: Indicates a successful or positive action
